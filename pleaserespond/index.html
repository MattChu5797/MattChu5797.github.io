How To Make A Bowl Of Cereal

    Supplies Needed

  1. Bowl
  2. Spoon
  3. Milk
  4. A Box of Cereal

Step 1- Gather All Of Your Supplies

Gather all of your supplies

Step 2- Get A Bowl And Add Cereal

First get your bowl and place it on the counter top or table. Then add cereal.

Step 3- Add Milk

1. Get milk 2. Remove cap 3. Pour onto cereal 4. put cap back on milk

Step 4- Add Spoon

Add a spoon to your bowl of cereal and enjoy!
